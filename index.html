Lucas Certo was born in July of '96, in Lisbon, in the same day as his mother. Studied architecture in Porto, did Erasmus and worked in Paris. Followig finishing his degree, moved to Brussels for two years, where he worked and did his first exhibtion/s. Back in his hometown, Lucas continues doing exhbitions of his of minimalistic aproach to art. The composition start as a line, that follows a flow that captures an image, and ends at thee point it started. This exercise of continuity and interruption brings a series of questions. Cilicity - what is it? Nature? Feliings? Time? space? Just a line that manage to find its way?
